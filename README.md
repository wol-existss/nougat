# Nougat, the all-in-one rope analyzer!
Nougat is comprised of a series of sensors used to detect defects and damage in rope.

## What's under the hood?

### On the hardware side, lots of sensors:
- An IR depth sensor for spotting larger defects, such as a damaged sheath
- A light and photodiode for spotting abrasion and tearing
- A microphone to detect "fuzzing"
- Rollers pressed against the rope used to detect hollow spots/core damage

### The brains:
- A Raspberry Pi Zero W
- LVGL as frontend UI

## Features
- Customizability of parameters on the device itself
- Intuitive controls
- Excellent battery life
- It's quick!
